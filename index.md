### About Me

Hi! I am a researcher working on training dynamics and model alignment at [London AI Safety Research Labs](https://lasrlabs.org/) with [Dima Krasheninnikov](https://krasheninnikov.github.io/) (Anthropic / Cambridge PhD). Before that, I was at the [Supervised Program for Alignment Research](https://sparai.org/), working on Mixture of Experts Interpretability with the [UK AI Security Institute](https://www.aisi.gov.uk/).

My interests include science of deep learning (pre-training dynamics, RL, and Continual Learning), interpretability generalisation to modern architectures (MoE, linear attention, distilled models), and the study of model personas and model organisms as tools for understanding how models learn behaviour.

### Work Experience
Previously, I worked for 3 years as a Machine Learning Engineer at [Fourth](https://www.fourth.com/solution/ai), where I improved Time-Series Models for large restaurant chains, and [STRAT7](https://strat7.com/specialisms/ai/), where I conducted NLP modelling on text reviews.

My Bachelor thesis at Uni Bonn was under [Prof. Teodora Boneva](https://teodoraboneva.com/) on the intersection of Statistics and Economics.
